## Features

- **4 Marketplaces, 1 Click** — Generate listings for Amazon, Etsy, Shopify, and eBay simultaneously
- **BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)** — Use your own Gemini API key for free unlimited AI generation with no daily limits
- **Marketplace-Specific SEO** — Optimized for Amazon A9, Etsy search, Shopify, and eBay Cassini algorithms
- **AI-Powered Optimization** — Titles, descriptions, tags, and keywords tailored to each platform's best practices
- **SEO Metadata Generation** — Auto-generated meta titles, descriptions, and keywords for your listings
- **CSV Export** — Download all generated listings for bulk upload to your seller accounts
- **Mobile Responsive** — Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

## How It Works

1. **Describe your product** — Enter your product name, category, and description
2. **AI generates listings** — QuickList AI creates optimized content for all 4 marketplaces
3. **Review & export** — Edit if needed, then export as CSV or copy directly
