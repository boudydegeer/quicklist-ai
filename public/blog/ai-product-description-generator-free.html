Tips for Getting the Best AI-Generated Listings

1. Be Specific in Your Input

Instead of "blue shirt," try "men's slim-fit Oxford cotton button-down shirt, navy blue, sizes S-XXL, machine washable." The more detail you provide, the better the output.

2. Include Your Target Audience

Mention who the product is for: "perfect for business casual office wear" or "ideal for outdoor enthusiasts." This helps the AI craft benefit-driven copy.

3. Highlight Unique Selling Points

What makes your product different? Handmade? Eco-friendly? Faster shipping? Include these differentiators in your input.

4. Review and Personalize

AI gives you an excellent starting point. Add your brand voice, specific measurements, or care instructions that only you know.
